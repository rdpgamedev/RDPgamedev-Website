Richard Pittman
Game Designer/Programmer

I'm Richard Pittman, a game designer and programmer. I've been playing games since I was a five years old, and have since had a great passion for the interactive art.

To me, interaction is the greatest dimension of entertainment -- greater than the spatial, aural, and temporal, the interactive dimension provides for a myriad of possibilities for the player's experience of the interactive medium, and we're only just now beginning to explore those possibilities.
