Richard Pittman
<Game Designer & Programmer>

I've been playing games since I was five years old, and have since had a great passion for the interactive art.

To me, interaction is the greatest dimension of entertainment; greater than the spatial, aural, and temporal, the interactive dimension provides for a myriad of possibilities for the player's experience of the interactive medium, and we're only just now beginning to explore those possibilities.

PROJECTS

Ariolimaxite

Ariolimaxite is a 2D, gridbased puzzle game centered around intense resource management, obfuscated information, and sci-fi themes. Drill down to the center of an asteroid to find fuel for your marooned spaceship.

Route 95

Route 95 is an audiovisual experience; be your own Desert Rocker on Route 95 and create songs that change the landscape around you with every note you play.

Magician's Hat vrOS

Magician's Hat is a proof of concept for a VR operating system that uses Leap Motion hand gestures and motion controls to connect the user with their computer.
